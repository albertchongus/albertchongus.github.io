Top 10 Origins of International Student in U.S.

In 2020, the total number of international students in U.S. is 914,095. They come from more than 200 contries/regions.
Here, the top 10 origins of the international students are displayed, as well as how the top 10 changes over the past 70 years.
The data is from https://opendoorsdata.org/data/international-students/all-places-of-origin/.
